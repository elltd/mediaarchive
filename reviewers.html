Designer who loves all media. He loves to watch movies with friends, listen to music too loudly, and buy more books than he can read (or fit on his shelf). Some of his favorite films include Broadcast News, Atonement, and God's Own Country.

Joel

Joel's passion for movies and TV shows began in childhood, often attempting (and failing) to sneak into R-rated movies at his local theater. Although he now enjoys media more responsibly, his irrational love for entertainment remains intact. Some of his favorite films include The Worst Person in the World, Network, and American Movie. In addition to studying and binge-watching shows, he spends the rest of his time listening to music, especially Kendrick Lamar, or cheering for his local(ish) sports team. (Go Nets!)

Some of my favorite musical artists are Belle and Sebastian, Of Montreal, Kings Of Convenience, Shintaro Sakamoto, and Alice Phoebe Lou.

Connor
